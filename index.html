About
An Italian FX artist driven by a genuine passion for the work. Great FX don't carry a film — but they can lift a great one to another level, and quietly rescue a struggling one. That challenge is exactly what I love about this work. My hope is that what I do inspires others to pursue the creative path they love, just as others inspired me to pursue mine.
